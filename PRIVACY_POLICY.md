# Privacy Policy for LisaSnap

**Last Updated:** February 6, 2026

## Introduction

LisaSnap ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Images**: Photos you take or select from your gallery for artifact analysis
- **Scan History**: Information about artifacts you've scanned (name, description, period, origin, etc.)
- **Collections**: Collections you create to organize your discoveries

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App usage statistics, features accessed
- **Analytics**: Anonymous usage patterns through Google Mobile Ads

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Analyze artifacts using AI technology (Anthropic Claude API)
- Store your scan history locally on your device
- Provide personalized collections and search functionality
- Process in-app purchases through RevenueCat
- Display relevant advertisements (free tier users)
- Improve app performance and user experience

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: All scan history, collections, and images are stored locally on your device using SQLite database
- **Cloud Processing**: Images are temporarily sent to Anthropic's servers for AI analysis, then immediately processed and not stored
- **No Account Required**: We do not collect or store personal identification information

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### Anthropic (Claude API)
- **Purpose**: AI-powered artifact analysis
- **Data Shared**: Images you scan
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://www.anthropic.com/privacy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)

### RevenueCat
- **Purpose**: In-app purchase and subscription management
- **Data Shared**: Purchase transactions, subscription status
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

### Google Mobile Ads
- **Purpose**: Displaying advertisements (free tier)
- **Data Shared**: Device information, advertising ID
- **Privacy Policy**: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device
- Images are encrypted during transmission to analysis servers
- We do not have access to your personal data or scan history
- You can delete your data at any time by deleting the app

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (stored locally on your device)
- Delete your data (by uninstalling the app or deleting individual items)
- Opt-out of advertisements by upgrading to Premium
- Request information about data processing

## Children's Privacy

LisaSnap is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: support@lisasnap.app

## Data Deletion

To delete your data:
1. Open LisaSnap app
2. Go to Settings
3. Delete individual scan history items, or
4. Uninstall the app to remove all local data

---

**Note**: This app stores all data locally on your device. We do not have access to your scan history, collections, or any personal information. Your privacy is fully in your control.
